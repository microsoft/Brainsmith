# Brainsmith - Coming Soon

!!! info "Launch Date: November 2025"
    Brainsmith is an open-source compiler that automates the design and implementation of neural network accelerators on FPGAs. Stay tuned for our official release!

---

## About

Brainsmith is developed through a collaboration between **Microsoft** and **AMD**, building upon industry-leading open-source frameworks [FINN](https://github.com/Xilinx/finn) and [QONNX](https://github.com/fastmachinelearning/qonnx) with a focus on modularity, extensibility, and automation.
